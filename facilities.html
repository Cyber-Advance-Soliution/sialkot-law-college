FACILITIES

Library

A fully functional library with a sufficient number of books and a reading room provides facility of extra reading to students and teachers. There are two independent libraries provided to students of Boys and Girls Wings. They are issued library cards using which they can borrow books for a specific time. Newspapers and magazines etc. are provided regularly in the reading room.

Playgrounds

DPS & C has vast playgrounds. School playgrounds and school playtime are vitally important to students for their fun, relaxation and their good health. School play grounds play an important role in their daily life for fulfilling their interest, development and learning needs. A New athletics-cum-cricket is being developed in Boys Section.

Security

To maintain the safety of students and school property DPS & C has sound security system. CCTV cameras have been installed in campus to ensure the better surveillance. Moreover, there is provision of trained armed guards at vantage points including roof tops.

Medical Facility

There is a dispensary in the institution which is run by a qualified para medical dispenser. It provides 1staid facility to students and staff. In case of severe sickness or injury, the patient is referred to DHQ Hospital.

TUTORIAL / HOUSE SYSTEM

For systematic and maximum participation of students, they are divided among four tutorial groups i.e. Jinnah House (Green), Iqbal House (Blue), Sir Sayed House (Yellow), Liaqat House (Red).There are regular House Meetings every Friday where students are given an opportunity to express their talent in speeches, debates, recitation, naat, national song and different sports etc. Inter House competitions are also conducted among the students throughout the year to polish their abilities in these fields.

LITERRARY ACTIVITIES

DPS & C T.T.Singh organizes a grand function every year. It is held to appreciate the students who showed extraordinary performance during the previous year in academics and co-curricular activities. The staff members who rendered valuable services to the institution in any respect are also awarded appreciation certificates. It is a time of great fun and enjoyment. It provides hundreds of students a chance to perform on stage.

ANNUAL SPORTS GALA

One week long games event is held every year in November by the name “Annual Sports Gala”. It provides the students an opportunity to prove their abilities in games like football, cricket, badminton, volley ball, table tennis, races, javelin throw, shot put, long jump, slow cycling etc. It is an inter-house event in which students contest for individual as well as overall prizes and trophies. Champion

Well Equipped Science and Computer Labs

Boys and girls Sections have adequately furnished and well-equipped laboratories for the subjects of Computer Science, Physics, Biology and Chemistry. The Junior Wing also has its independent computer lab. They cater the needs of students at secondary level sufficiently. The students frequently visit these labs for practical lessons. It is greatly helpful in concreting the concepts.

SCIENCE / CULTURAL EXHIBITION

Once in a year, a science/cultural exhibition is held to engage students in preparing science and cultural models. It promotes practical knowledge of science among students. Parents are invited to observe and enjoy the display.

FAREWELL GATHERING & COLOUR DAY

School organizes farewell party every year to final year students. This day gives an opportunity to students to celebrate something with friends, faculty and juniors.Colour day is celebrated every year in Junior Wing and Girls Wing where students not only enjoy but also learn the concept of sharing and caring.

DEBATING & NAAT & QIRAT COMPETITIONS

Naat and Qirat Competition is held every year. Moreover, Seerat Conference is also held on the occasion to highlight the life and achievements of Holy Prophet (P.B.U.H).
