ADMISSION PROCEDURE

The completed Admission Form along with copies of

  1. Required Documents:
    • I) Student CNIC, Father/Guardian CNIC (6 Copies) (In case of Under 18 B.Form)
    • II) 6 Pictures Passport size with Blue Background
    • III) Matric Result Card Copies (06 Copies)
    • IV) Intermediate Result Card (06 Copies) Equivalence Certificate in case of O'A Level
    • V) HEC Law Admission Test Result Card (06 Copies)
  2. Submission of Admission/Registration Fee (Non Refundable)
  3. Short Listening For Interview
  4. Admission Offer Letter
  5. Deposit of Fee (After the Admission Form has been processed, 1st Installment of college Tuition Fee paid within 3 days of the date of offer. If, within 3 days, enrolment is not confirmed, the admission place is offered to another candidate)
